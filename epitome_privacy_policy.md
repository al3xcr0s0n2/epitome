**Last updated: July 2026**

---

## Who we are

Epitome is an emotional intelligence logging application created and owned by Alexander Croson. This privacy policy explains how Epitome handles your personal data.

---

## The short version

Your data never leaves your device. Epitome does not collect, transmit, store or share any of your personal data with any third party, server, or cloud service. Everything you record stays on your phone.

---

## What data Epitome collects

When you use Epitome, the following data is recorded and stored **locally on your device only**:

- **Date and time** of each log entry
- **Location** — only when you choose to fetch it by tapping the location field. This uses your device's GPS and is stored as a place name only. Your location is never transmitted anywhere.
- **Company** — any names or notes you choose to enter
- **Photographs** — taken within the app and stored on your device
- **Audio recordings** — recorded within the app and stored on your device
- **Drawings** — created within the app and stored on your device
- **Emotional Intelligence ratings** — your self-assessed EQ scores across the five Goleman pillars
- **EQ average scores** — calculated from your ratings

---

## What data Epitome does NOT collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information
- We do not require you to create an account
- We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
- We do not transmit any data over the internet
- We do not store any data on external servers
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not use cookies

---

## Where your data is stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's AsyncStorage system. It remains there until you choose to delete individual entries, or until you uninstall the app.

---

## Exporting your data

Epitome provides an **Export** feature that allows you to save your data as a JSON file to your device's Downloads folder. This is for your own backup purposes only. You control where this file goes. We recommend transferring it via USB cable to your personal computer — no internet connection is required or used during this process.

---

## Permissions

Epitome requests the following device permissions:

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| **Camera** | To capture photographs within log entries |
| **Microphone** | To record audio clips within log entries |
| **Location** | To fetch your current location when requested |
| **Storage** | To save exported data to your Downloads folder |

You may deny any of these permissions. The app will continue to function — you simply won't be able to use the features that require them.

---

## Children's privacy

Epitome is intended for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.
